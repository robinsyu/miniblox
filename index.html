Frequently Asked Questions

Is MiniBlox free to play?

Yes! MiniBlox is completely free to play directly in your browser without downloads or installations.

Can I play MiniBlox on mobile devices?

Yes, MiniBlox is designed to be responsive and works well on both desktop and mobile devices with touch controls.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required to start playing, though creating one allows you to save your progress and access additional features.

Can I play with my friends?

Yes! MiniBlox supports multiplayer functionality so you can build and explore with friends in shared worlds.

Are there regular updates?

Yes, we regularly update MiniBlox with new features, blocks, and game modes to keep the experience fresh and exciting.
